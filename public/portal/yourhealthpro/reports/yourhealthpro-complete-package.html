What Is YourHealthPro?

YHP is a DoorDash-style marketplace for mobile healthcare providers — built by Ed Cutler, founder of Alpha Phlebotomy Group, who has nearly 30 years in mobile phlebotomy and operates a network of 6,000+ certified phlebotomists across all 50 states.

The platform is feature-rich but stuck in pre-launch with no active developers. VSP is delivering the platform to production via a revenue share model, unlocking near-immediate B2C revenue from existing manual volume, then transitioning to a standard retainer once the business is generating cash.

Bottom line: The business already exists. The revenue is already flowing manually. The app just has to turn on.

Ed already has 1,000+ providers in his network. He only needs 10% conversion. He gets daily inbound from providers asking how to grow their business — demand is organic.

B2C Marketplace (Primary)

  • Platform charges a 15% convenience fee on each transaction
  • Phlebotomist sets their own rate
  • Patient pays platform fee + provider rate directly
  • Example: Patient pays ~$90 direct vs. ~$149 when Alpha is the middleman
  • ~$59/patient savings = compelling value prop for patients

B2B Managed Engagements

  • Alpha charges a set fee to the client and pays providers a set rate
  • Alpha keeps the margin
  • Used for clinical trials, genomic testing, high-touch managed engagements
  • Alpha currently "barely breaking even" on phlebotomy side but gaining new accounts
