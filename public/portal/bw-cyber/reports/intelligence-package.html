Microsoft Purview DSPM for AI — The Oversharing Scanner
Cost: Included in M365 E5 | 90-day free trial available for assessments

The closest thing to "Tenable for AI" in the Microsoft stack. DSPM for AI automatically identifies the top 100 SharePoint sites by Copilot usage, flags oversharing risks, enables item-level investigation, and monitors Copilot prompts/responses for sensitive data exposure.

The payroll file scenario: DSPM runs a Data Risk Assessment that surfaces exactly the problem Michael described — files with sensitive data (payroll, PII, financial records) accessible to Copilot due to misconfigured SharePoint permissions. Flags "Everyone except external users" access, broad security groups, and anyone-links.

3
SharePoint Advanced Management + Compliance Manager (NIST AI RMF)
Cost: Included with M365 Copilot license | $3/user/month add-on for non-Copilot tenants

SharePoint Advanced Management (SAM) was specifically built for the Copilot oversharing problem: Site Permissions Reports, Data Access Governance Reports (who has access to what), Restricted Access Control (one-click lockdown), and Content Discovery Controls (prevent sites from Copilot search).

Compliance Manager (NIST AI RMF) provides regulatory assessment templates mapping M365 configurations to recognized frameworks — NIST AI RMF (referenced by the SEC), EU AI Act (hard deadline August 2, 2026), and ISO/IEC 42001. Produces a percentage compliance score with specific control gaps highlighted. Runs in under an hour. Output is a regulatory gap report a CCO can hand directly to an SEC examiner.

Layer 4: Shadow AI Detection — Microsoft Defender for Cloud Apps

For hedge funds specifically: Defender for Cloud Apps can surface if employees are uploading client data, trading strategies, or financial models to ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, etc. — a direct SEC/FINRA compliance risk.

Discovers 400+ AI/GenAI apps in use across the organization. Assigns each app a risk score (1–10), shows usage volume, allows admin to tag apps as Sanctioned, Monitored, or Unsanctioned. Available with E3 (basic) or E5/MDCA (~$3.50/user/month) for full capabilities. Output: the "Shadow AI Inventory" section of the assessment.
