Current Workflow

The Forensic Evaluation Process

Four phases, each with distinct AI opportunities. This is the workflow the MVP must support.

1

Records Review

Ingest case documents (PDFs, scanned docs from 1980s, handwritten police reports). Cross-reference, flag gaps and inconsistencies.

2

Clinical Interview

1–4 hours face-to-face. Must capture verbatim quotes for court citation. Currently types manually because transcription tools are unreliable.

3

Report Writing

15–22+ page structured report: history, interview, tests, analysis, conclusions. Every statement traceable to source material.

4

Testimony

Defend every statement under oath. Opposing counsel challenges citations. Zero tolerance for fabrication or hallucination.

Proposed MVP

Three Core Modules

Agreed on the April 1 discovery call. Fixed-price, deliverable-based engagement.

Records Review Assistance

  • Ingest case documents (PDF, scanned, handwritten)
  • Summarize and cross-reference across sources
  • Flag gaps and inconsistencies
  • Track every citation back to source document

Interview Recording + Transcription

  • Record 1–4 hour clinical interviews with consent
  • Accurate, context-aware transcription
  • Extract verbatim quotes for report insertion
  • Searchable transcript with timestamps

Report Drafting

  • Structured forensic evaluation report template
  • Traceable citations — non-negotiable
  • History section synthesis from records
  • "Beyond reproach" for court submission
